Isaac Newton

Born: January 4, 1643

Died: March 31, 1727

Isaac Newton was a mathematician, physicist, astronomer, and author who is widely recognized as one of the most influential scientists of all time. He formulated the laws of motion and universal gravitation, which laid the foundation for classical mechanics.
